Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation menu, fill in your email and password, and follow the verification steps.

How do I cast my vote?

After logging in, go to the "Vote" page, select your preferred candidate, and click "Submit Vote".

Is my vote secure?

Yes, all votes are encrypted and stored securely. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I change my vote?

No, once submitted, votes cannot be changed. Please verify your selection before submitting.
